=== YOUR TASK ===
You must produce ALL sections below. Be ruthless and specific.

## 1. BREAK-IT LIST
List 5-10 specific scenarios that would cause this plan to fail. For each:
- **Scenario**: What happens? (Be concrete, not abstract)
- **Probability**: How likely is this? (with brief justification)
- **Impact**: How bad would it be?
- **Why the plan doesn't address it**: What gap in the analysis allows this?

Prioritize: Start with the most dangerous (high probability × high impact) scenarios.

## 2. LOGIC AUDIT
Check the reasoning chain for:
- **Unstated assumptions**: What is taken for granted but might not be true?
- **Logical leaps**: Where does the argument skip steps?
- **Confirmation bias**: Where did both advisors agree too easily? (Unanimous agreement on uncertain topics is suspicious)
- **Anchoring**: Did early-round framing unduly constrain later thinking?

## 3. MISSING INFORMATION
What critical information is needed but wasn't gathered?
- For each missing piece: What is it? Why does it matter? How could it change the conclusion?
- Rate each: MUST-HAVE (decision changes without it) vs. NICE-TO-HAVE (decision is better with it)

## 4. MINORITY REPORT
Is there a contrarian position that was too quickly dismissed during debate?
- State the contrarian view
- Give the strongest 2-3 arguments FOR it
- Under what conditions would this contrarian view actually be correct?

## 5. IMPLEMENTATION RISKS
Beyond the plan's content, what can go wrong in execution?
- Organizational / political risks
- Resource / timeline risks
- Dependency / sequencing risks
- Communication / coordination risks

## 6. PATCHES
For each issue identified above, propose a SPECIFIC fix:
- Issue → Patch (concrete change to the plan)
- Prioritize: CRITICAL (must fix before proceeding) vs. RECOMMENDED (should fix) vs. OPTIONAL (nice to have)

## 7. AUDIT VERDICT
In 5-10 lines, give your overall assessment:
- Is the plan safe to execute as-is?
- What are the 1-3 MUST-FIX items before proceeding?
- What is your confidence level in the plan? (1-10 scale with justification)
- Final recommendation: APPROVE / APPROVE WITH CONDITIONS / REVISE / REJECT
